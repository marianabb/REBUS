What is ReBuS ... and why?

 

ReBuS stands for Resources for Bulgarian Studies. The idea is to create a common playground for educators and researchers around the world who are working with different aspects of the Bulgarian language, literature and culture. Together we create the ReBuS data base, and by doing so, we can all share each others experiences in teaching courses or our latest research results.

In order to add information to the data base one needs to be a registered user. Please take 5 minutes to register, so that you are able to join the ReBuS community.
